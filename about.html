How We Got Here

A Platform Built from a Genuine Creative Pain

Graphiqz started in 2026 when our co-founders — a motion designer and a machine learning engineer — sat down over a problem: why does creating a 10-second animation take 4 hours in professional tools?

They believed that large language models, combined with structured canvas rendering pipelines, could dramatically compress that timeline. The first prototype generated a particle animation from a prompt in 45 seconds. That moment changed everything.

Today, Graphiqz serves over 12,000 creators globally — from YouTubers and educators to startup founders and enterprise marketing teams — all using AI-generated motion graphics to bring their ideas to life instantly.

We're a small, remote-first team distributed across Karachi, London, Singapore, and San Francisco. We ship fast, listen to our users, and obsess over output quality.

Our Core Values

Every second you wait is a second your idea isn't alive. We obsessively optimize generation time because creative momentum is sacred.

AI-generated doesn't mean low quality. We've engineered our pipeline so every output is production-grade — smooth, polished, and ready to use.
